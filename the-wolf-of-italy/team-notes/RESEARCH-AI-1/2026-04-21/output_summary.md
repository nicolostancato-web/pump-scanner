## 🚀 The Solution (Minimal MVP)

**What We Build:**
1. **MCP Memory Plugin** — Intercepts API calls, caches prompts, deduplicates inputs
2. **Token Dashboard** — Shows real-time waste metrics + savings estimate
3. **Billing Integration** — Monthly billing, accept SOL for crypto users
4. **Integrations** — OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), LLaMA-based APIs

**How It Works:**
- Customer deploys our plugin in their AI stack
- Automatically caches frequently-used prompts and context
- Real savings: 25–40% token reduction (proven by YourMemory)
- Customer pays 10-20% of monthly savings OR flat $500–$2K/month fee

**Why It Wins:**
- ✅ Proven demand (YourMemory exists, customers want it)
- ✅ Low build complexity (MCP standard already exists)
- ✅ Immediate ROI (customers save money immediately)
- ✅ Sticky product (integrated into dev workflow)
- ✅ Scalable revenue (works with any API spend)
